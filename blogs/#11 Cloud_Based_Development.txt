Daily blog #11: Cloud-Based Development- from Dream to Reality

Author: Mike Nikles

Source: https://www.infoq.com/articles/cloud-based-development/

Cloud application development refers to the process by which cloud-based apps are built by the use of DevOps 
practices and tools. It involves different stages of software development.

Software as a service solution quickly brought a significant shift as all SDLC stages are performed in the cloud 
except for the implementation stage of the cycle which is considered a laggard.

The key takeaway from this article to Kweli Capital is that we could leverage platforms and SaaS offerings for the 
majority of the SDCL.
